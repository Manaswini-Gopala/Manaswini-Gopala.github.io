Strategic Research Analyst

Global supply chains operate under constant uncertainty, where disruptions can have real financial and operational consequences. To better understand these risks, I analyze SEC 10-Q and 10-K filings from 40+ global companies and build a structured risk taxonomy that captures both operational and financial exposures. I look for recurring themes, emerging patterns, and differences in how companies communicate material issues.
I turn this analysis into dashboards that track risk frequency, severity, and financial relevance, making it easier to compare companies and spot high-impact vulnerabilities. Alongside this, I am prototyping a Python-based NLP tool to automatically extract risk disclosures from filings, reducing manual review time on data collection. This work helps bring clarity to complex risk information and supports more informed, forward-looking prioritization.
